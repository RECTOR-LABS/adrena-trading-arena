## Core Challenge

Adrena seeks design and development of "a new competition module or meaningful improvement" to its trading competition infrastructure. The platform generated 50% of 2025 trading volume through competitions, which currently include P&L leaderboards, quests, streaks, and raffles.

## Required Deliverables

### 1. Competition Design Document

- Proposed format (new type or improvement)

- Integration approach with existing systems
- Competitive advantages versus rival perp DEXs
- Edge case and fraud prevention strategies

### 2. Working Prototype/Implementation

- Functional, reviewable code
- Integration with Adrena systems (team coordination required)
- Deployment and configuration documentation

### 3. Testing & Feedback

- Test competition with small user group
- Documented feedback and results
- Iteration recommendations
